# Film Streamer

A comprehensive web application to stream or download video files, with advanced features for finding and playing movies from various sources.

## 🚀 Features

- **Local Films**: Stream or download video files from the `films/` folder
- **Play Any URL**: Stream from direct video links, YouTube, or Vimeo
- **CORS Proxy**: Bypass cross-origin restrictions for external videos [citation:1][citation:5][citation:9]
- **Torrent Search**: Find movies on YTS and get torrent links [citation:2][citation:10]
- **Free Streaming Guide**: Directory of legitimate free ad-supported services [citation:4][citation:8]
- **Streaming Availability API** (coming soon): Check where movies are available [citation:3]
- **Docker Support**: Easy deployment with containers
